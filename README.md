# WOO-Commerce-Analysis
Developed interactive Power BI dashboards with charts and filters to track and visualize business performance. Prepared and cleaned data using Power Query and created DAX formulas to calculate key metrics such as revenue and growth. Delivered insights that helped identify sales trends, top-performing products, and areas for improvement.


 Key Features 

Interactive Dashboards:
Created multiple interactive visuals to analyze sales, profit, and customer data across different product categories and time periods.

Dynamic KPIs:
Added real-time summary cards for:

Total Sales

Total Profit

Total Orders

Average Order Value

Sales Trends:
Line and bar charts show monthly and quarterly sales performance, helping identify growth patterns and slow periods.

Category & Product Analysis:
Visualized Top-Selling Categories and Products with dynamic filters for region, time, and product type.

Customer Insights:
Segmented data by customer region, gender, and age group to analyze purchasing behavior.

Profitability Breakdown:
Included visuals comparing Revenue vs. Profit by product, category, and region for better business decisions.

Interactive Filters & Slicers:
Easy-to-use filters for Year, Region, Category, and Customer Segment allow users to explore the data freely.

Clean and Modern Design:
Used professional color themes, icons, and layout to ensure dashboards are visually appealing and easy to read.

 Tool Used: Power BI Desktop
 Dataset: WooCommerce Sales Data (Excel/CSV format)
